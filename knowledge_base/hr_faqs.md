# HR Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I request time off?
Time off can be requested through the Workday portal under "Time & Absence". Please give your manager at least 2 weeks' notice for vacations longer than 3 days.

## What are the observed holidays?
We observe the standard federal holidays: New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (and the day after), and Christmas Day.

## When do benefits kick in?
Health, dental, and vision benefits begin on your first day of employment.

## How do I enroll in the 401(k) plan?
You will receive an email from our provider, Fidelity, within your first week. We match 100% of the first 4% of your contributions.
