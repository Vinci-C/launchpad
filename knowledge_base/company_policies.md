# Company Policies

## Welcome to the Company
Welcome to Acme Corp! We are thrilled to have you here. This document outlines our basic policies.

## Remote Work
- Employees are allowed to work remotely 2 days a week (Tuesday and Thursday are popular).
- Core hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in your local timezone.

## Expense Policy
- Meals during business travel are covered up to $75 per day.
- Software subscriptions must be approved by your manager via the IT portal.

## Code of Conduct
We expect all employees to treat each other with respect. Harassment or discrimination of any kind is strictly prohibited.
